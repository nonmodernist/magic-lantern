üìñ About Magic Lantern

Magic Lantern transforms weeks of manual searching into hours of automated discovery across the Media History Digital Library's Lantern search platform.

What It Does

This tool generates 15+ intelligent search queries per film, executes them gracefully with proper rate limiting, deduplicates results, and presents them ranked by relevance.

How to Use

  1. Prepare a CSV file with columns: title, year, author, director, studio
  2. Select your CSV using the file picker
  3. Choose a corpus size and research profile
  4. Click "Execute Search" and let Magic Lantern work its magic
  5. Review your results and export as needed

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues:

  • Ensure Lantern (lantern.mediahist.org) is accessible
  • Check your CSV formatting matches the expected columns
  • Try the "Test" corpus size first
